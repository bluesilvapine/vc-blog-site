My typing journey started on a standard rubber dome keyboard. But once I discovered mechanical keyboards, there was no going back.

### Layouts: 60% vs. TKL vs. Split

*   **TKL (Tenkeyless)**: Standard layout minus the numpad. A safe, comfortable choice.
*   **60% Layout**: Highly compact, mapping arrows and functions to function layers. Excellent for keeping hands close to the home row.
*   **Split Ergonomic**: Splitting the board into two halves helps align wrists and shoulders naturally, reducing fatigue.

For writing code, a 65% split layout is my absolute favorite, offering dedicated arrow keys while keeping a minimal footprint.
