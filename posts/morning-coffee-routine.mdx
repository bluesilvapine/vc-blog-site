Before the emails start flooding in, before the stand-up meetings, and before the terminal windows open, there is the coffee.

For many, coffee is pure fuel—a caffeine hit delivered via a pod machine or a quick stop at a drive-thru. But for me, the manual process of brewing a pour-over is a meditative transition from sleep to work.

### The Recipe

I follow a standard 1:16 ratio. For a single mug, that translates to:
*   **15g** of freshly ground coffee (medium-coarse, like sea salt).
*   **240g** of water heated to exactly 93°C (200°F).
